Seattle's British Pub.

Since 1995 a little bit of Britain in Seattle. Located in the Fremont area of Seattle. Serving cocktails, fine ales and great food. Enjoy our large outdoor patios for lunch, dinner and drinks. A few drinks with your friends, a big night out, football, chips, Birds and Blokes... The G&D is the home for the masses.

The home of football

The George & Dragon is the go-to establishment for all things football. We show numerous English Premier League games each week.
